Our Journey

From a small repair shop to Bangalore's most trusted Apple service center

The Beginning (2015)

It all started when our founder, Vinod Kumar, was frustrated with the poor quality Apple repair services available in Bangalore. As an Apple enthusiast himself, he noticed that most repair shops used inferior parts and lacked proper expertise.

Building Excellence (2016-2019)

We invested heavily in Apple-certified training programs and established partnerships with authorized component suppliers. Our commitment to using only genuine parts and providing transparent service quickly gained customer trust.

Expansion & Innovation (2020-Present)

Today, we've expanded our services across Bangalore with doorstep repair, same-day service, and industry-leading warranties. We've successfully repaired over 15,000 Apple devices and maintain a 95% success rate.
